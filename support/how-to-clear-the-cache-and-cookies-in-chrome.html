How to clear the cache and cookies in Chrome

"Cached" data can easily take up a lot of storage space on your device. These caches of data are stored on your hard disk so that future requests for the data can be served faster. Cookies are small files that hold data specific to a particular client and website. They can be accessed by web servers to deliver a page tailored to the user. Follow the step by step instructions below to delete the cache and cookies when using Google Chrome on Windows.

Step 1:

  • Click on the Menu button, located on the upper right corner.
  • Select History from the drop-down menu.

Step 2:

  • On the left side column, click on Clear all browsing data.

Step 3:

  • Choose From the beginning of time and click on Clear browsing data.
  • Note: Make sure the boxes next to Cookies and other site data and Cached images and files are checked.
