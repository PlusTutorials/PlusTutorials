How to clear the cache and cookies in Firefox

"Cached" data can easily take up a lot of storage space on your device. These caches of data(images, scripts, and other parts of a website) are stored on your hard disk so that future requests for the data can be served faster.

Cookies are small files that hold data specific to a particular client and website. They can be accessed by web servers to deliver a page tailored to the user. Follow the step by step instructions in this article to delete the cache and cookies when using Mozilla Firefox.

Note: Clearing all cookies and site data stored by your web browser may sign you out of websites and remove offline web content. Clearing cache data will not affect your logins.

Step 1:

  • Click on the Menu button, located on the upper right corner.
  • Select Options.

Step 2:

  • In the Privacy & Security section, under Cookies and Site Data click on Clear Data button.

Step 3:

  • Click on Clear.
  • Note: Make sure the boxes next to Cookies and Site Data and Cached Web Content are checked.
