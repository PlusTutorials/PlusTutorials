How to clear the cache and cookies in Edge

"Cached" data can easily take up a lot of storage space on your device. These caches of data are stored on your hard disk so that future requests for the data can be served faster. Cookies are small files that hold data specific to a particular client and website. They can be accessed by web servers to deliver a page tailored to the user. Follow the step by step instructions below to delete the cache and cookies when using Microsoft Edge.

Step 1:

  • Click on the Menu button, located on the upper right corner.

Step 2:

  • Click on Settings.

Step 3:

  • Under Clear browsing data, click on Choose what to clear.

Step 4:

  • Check the boxes next to Cookies and saved website data and Cached data and files.
  • Click on Clear
